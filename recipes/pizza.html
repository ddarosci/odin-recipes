Ingredients

  • Dough
    • Flour
    • Yeast
    • Salt
    • Water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella di buffala or fior di latte

    • Steps

    1. Mix the flour, yeast, salt, and water to make the dough.
    2. Let it rest from 24 to 48 hours
    3. Roll the dough into a pizza shape
    4. Add the tomato sauce and the mozzarella
    5. Cook it
